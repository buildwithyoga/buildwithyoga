Why it stays affordable

Unlike most agencies that host your site on their own infrastructure and start charging monthly or annual “maintenance” fees after the first free year, your website is built on Cloudflare Pages, a free hosting platform — so once it's built, you will never receive a hosting bill.

  • •One-time setup, not a subscription. No recurring fees, and no risk of your site going down if a payment is missed.
  • •Only your domain renews annually — e.g. yourstudio.in — and it's billed directly by the domain registrar, not by me.
  • •If the domain lapses, only that custom address stops resolving — your website itself stays live and safe.
  • •Renewal is your choice. Do it yourself anytime, or I can handle it.
  • •No hidden charges. Any third-party costs such as payment gateways, premium plugins, hosting upgrades or SMS/WhatsApp services will always be discussed and approved before implementation.
  • •Ongoing assistance. Need a content update, a new feature, or help renewing your domain later? Just get back to me, and we'll agree on fair, scope-based pricing before any work begins.
