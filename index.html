Bringing technology and yoga together

After 7+ years in software engineering, I completed my M.Sc. in Yoga Therapy from S-VYASA. It was a journey that changed how I see both fields — technology isn't just about efficiency, and yoga isn't just about postures. Both, at their best, are about creating the right conditions for growth.

Along the way, I noticed something that didn't sit right with me: yoga teachers — people devoted to service (seva) — were often stuck with outdated websites, or locked into recurring subscription fees just to keep a basic page alive. Many were paying month after month for tools that barely reflected the depth of what they teach.

That felt like the opposite of yoga's spirit of freedom and non-attachment (aparigraha). Your practice teaches you to let go of what doesn't serve you — your website shouldn't be one more thing quietly draining your energy and income every month.

So I started building websites made specifically for the yoga community — ones you truly own, with no strings attached, and no dependency on me or anyone else to keep them running.
